## ResponsiveEmbed Component

The ResponsiveEmbed component allows you to embed content with proper aspect ratios:

<ResponsiveEmbed src="https://giphy.com/embed/gtakVlnStZUbe" ratio="4:3" />

You can also embed GIFs with different aspect ratios:

<ResponsiveEmbed src="https://giphy.com/embed/VqleJG2rBop7W" ratio="1:1" />

## Break Component

The Break component provides line breaks in MDX content:

This is the first line.<Break/>
This is the second line after a break.

Normal paragraph continues here.
